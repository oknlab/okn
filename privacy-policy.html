OKNLAB ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website and use our services, how we use it, and how we protect it. By accessing oknlab.com, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you provide to us directly, and information collected automatically as you interact with our platform.

Information You Provide

  • Email address when you subscribe to our newsletter or waitlist

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • IP address and approximate geographic location (country/region level)
  • Pages viewed, time on page, referral URLs, and click interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following legitimate purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and AI products
  • To send updates, release notes, and early-access announcements you have opted into
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To analyze aggregate usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security vulnerabilities
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

We will not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable under GDPR or similar frameworks, we process personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — where you have opted in, such as subscribing to our mailing list
  • Contractual necessity — to perform services you have requested
  • Legitimate interests — for product analytics and security monitoring
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the platform, subject to confidentiality agreements. These include:

We may also disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of OKNLAB, our users, or the public.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve site functionality and understand usage patterns. Types include:

  • Essential cookies — required for basic site operation
  • Analytics cookies — used to understand aggregate traffic patterns

You may disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that some features may not function correctly without them.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Newsletter subscriber email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is anonymized after 26 months.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@oknlab.slmail.me. We will respond within 30 days.

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
